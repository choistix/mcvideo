Highsnobiety Issue 019 - Coach x Michael B. Jordan Naruto Collaboration GIFs

In parallel to the launch of the Coach x Michael B. Jordan Naruto Collaboration Line, I was commissioned to create four animated “Anime inspired” GIFs utilizing already shot BTS B-roll footage from the Highsnobiety Cover Photoshoot.

While I would have loved to have shot the footage specifically with these animations in mind, I do believe that these turned out pretty well, and pays great homage to classic anime.
